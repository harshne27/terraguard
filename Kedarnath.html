The Kedarnath disaster of June 16-17, 2013, was one of the worst natural calamities in the Indian Himalayas. Triggered by unprecedented cloudbursts and heavy rainfall, devastating flash floods and landslides destroyed the pilgrimage town and surrounding villages in Uttarakhand.

  • Date: June 16-17, 2013
  • State: Uttarakhand (Rudraprayag District)
  • Deaths: 5,000+ confirmed
  • Missing: Over 1,000 people
  • Rainfall: 375 mm in 24 hours
  • Displaced: 100,000+ people
